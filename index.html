FAQs

What is the 4-STEP-TPS program?

The 4-STEP-TPS (Screening, Treatment, Effective Management, and Prevention) program is a comprehensive digital wellness training designed to help individuals overcome social media addiction in just 6 hours. It addresses the factors that lead to social media addiction and provides personalized strategies for effective management and prevention.

Who can benefit from the 4-STEP-TPS program?

Anyone who feels overwhelmed by their social media usage or experiences symptoms of social media addiction, such as FoMO (Fear of Missing Out), anxiety, or decreased productivity, can benefit from this program. It's suitable for individuals of all ages and backgrounds looking to improve their digital wellness.

What does the 6-hour training entail?

The 6-hour training is divided into four main steps: Screening, Assessment, and Psychoeducation; Treatment, Conceptualization, and Planning; Effective Management Strategies; and Prevention (Lapse-Relapse). Each step includes interactive sessions, practical exercises, and personalized plans to help you gain control over your social media habits.

How can I access the 4-STEP-TPS program?

You can access the 4-STEP-TPS program by signing up through our website. Once enrolled, you'll receive all the necessary materials and instructions to start your training. The program is designed to be tailored and flexible for every person.
